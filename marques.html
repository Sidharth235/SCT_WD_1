Legacy Milestones

1906 – Rolls-Royce is Born

Charles Rolls and Henry Royce unveil the Silver Ghost, setting the standard for automotive refinement.

1913 – Aston Martin Founded

Founded by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford, Aston Martin soon became a British icon of speed and elegance.

1931 – Birth of the Bentley Boys

Racing gentlemen made Bentley a household name by dominating Le Mans in the roaring twenties.

1947 – Ferrari Roars to Life

Enzo Ferrari creates the first car under his own name: the 125 S. A legend begins.
